Lion

Practically the only one of the great cats living in flocks. We all know them as king of animals.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Lenght: 1.6 - 2.5 m without tail
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Africa and Eurasia

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth, and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold.
