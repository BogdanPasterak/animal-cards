Lion

Practically the only one of the great cats living in flocks. We all know them as king of animals.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Lenght: 1.6-2.5 m less tail
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Africa and Eurasia

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth, and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold.

Human

Live in big packs, most often in the town area. They communicate by sound, gestures and the internet.

  • Scientific Name: Homo sapiens
  • Average Lenght: 1.7 meter
  • Average Lifespan: 79 years
  • Habitat: Earth and more

At the moment they are the most dominant specie. They live on all of the continents, inside and on the water and inside and beyond the atmosphere.
